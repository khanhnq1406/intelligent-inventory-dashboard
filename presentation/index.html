Lessons Learned

Architecture first,
everything else follows

Biggest Challenge

Getting the initial architecture, patterns, and coding style right. If you go in the wrong direction at this stage, every implementation built on top inherits those problems.

This is where AI collaboration helped the most — evaluating trade-offs systematically rather than going with first instinct.

Key Takeaways

  • ✓ Invest heavily in upfront design — it saves far more time during implementation
  • ✓ AI as a sparring partner for architecture — forces you to articulate your reasoning
  • ✓ Keep humans in the loop — AI generates options, but decisions shape the app
  • ✓ Contract-first development eliminates entire categories of bugs
